Population health management (PHM) is a proactive, people-focused approach that uses data to improve the health and well-being of specific groups. It takes into account differences in risk, needs, and social factors within communities and adjusts services accordingly. Rather than applying a one-size-fits-all model, PHM enables primary care to deliver targeted, equitable interventions.
